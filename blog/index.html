Welcome

A new Web presence for the Cambridge CP

August 18, 2016 Phil Y.

Welcome to the official blog of the Cambridge Branch of the Communist Party of Britain. We are a group of Marxist-Leninist activists fighting for a socialist Britain. This blog aims to be a collection of writings from our branch members (with the potential to include guest writers) on current British and international politics from a far-left and anti-imperialist perspective. We hope that you find this blog to be informative and stimulating.

About

We are the Cambridge Branch of the Communist Party of Britain, the UK's largest Marxist-Leninist party. We fight for the construction of a socialist, federal British republic. Our branch meets at 1930 on the first Friday of every month, at the Memorial (Unitarian) Church, Emmanuel Road, Cambridge. For more information, contact us at info@cambridgecommunist.party
