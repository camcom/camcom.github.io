About Us

We are the Cambridge Branch of the Communist Party of Britain, the UK’s largest Marxist-Leninist party.

We fight for the construction of a socialist, federal British republic, following our party programme, Britain’s Road to Socialism.

Our branch meets at 1930 on the first Friday of every month, at the Memorial (Unitarian) Church, Emmanuel Road, Cambridge.

For more information, contact us at info@cambridgecommunist.party.
