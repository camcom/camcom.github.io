Welcome

A new Web presence for the Cambridge CP

August 18, 2016 Phil Y.

Welcome to the official blog of the Cambridge Branch of the Communist Party of Britain. We are a group of Marxist-Leninist activists fighting for a socialist Britain. This blog constitutes a collection of writings by our branch members (with the potential to include guest writers) on current British and international politics from a far-left and anti-imperialist perspective. We hope that you find this blog to be informative and stimulating. If you have any questions or comments, please get in touch via e-mail, Facebook or Twitter, or come along to one of our branch meetings, which are held at 1930 on the first Friday of every month, at the Memorial (Unitarian) Church, Emmanuel Road, Cambridge.

About

We are the Cambridge Branch of the Communist Party of Britain, the UK's largest Marxist-Leninist party. We fight for the construction of a socialist, federal British republic. Our branch meets at 1930 on the first Friday of every month, at the Memorial (Unitarian) Church, Emmanuel Road, Cambridge. For more information, contact us at info@cambridgecommunist.party
